Why select Bangladesh as a travel destination

Bangladesh remains unnoticed despite its natural beauty. It's a country with rich and diverse culture. As a travel destination Bangladesh has a lot to offer. There beaches for beach lovers, mountains for mountain lovers, historical sites for those who are interested in knowing the rich history of this county. Not only does Bangladesh offer an unforgettable experience in terms of nature the interaction with locals will also be something worth to remember for the travelers.
